About Us

Who We Are...

The Temple Inn & Suites is a hotel chain headquartered in Bethesda, MD that builds and manages specialized hotel accommodations near temple locations of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints around the United States. The owner-operated company caters to temple workers, patrons, and their families by providing specialized services and accommodations that meet the needs of those patrons. The hotel accommodations and services are available to anyone willing to abide by the terms and policies outlined by the company. Our goal is to promote and provide specialized services that meet the needs of temple patrons who come to serve in the temple or who participate in events such as sealings, weddings, receptions, and youth trips.

Why The Olive Branch?

After so many days of travel, Noah and his family were anxious to find a place to land. Not only were they ready to get out of the boat and stretch their legs, they were hoping to find a safe place that would enable them to build an altar and worship. In Genesis chapter 8 we read how Noah sent forth a dove to see if the waters had abated. After each reconnaissance mission, the weary dove returned home to the ark. Finally the dove is sent out and brings back good news in the form of an olive branch, so Noah knew that the waters were abated from off the earth. We at Temple Inn & Suites want to be that place of rest for weary travelers looking for an opportunity to worship at the altars of the temple.
