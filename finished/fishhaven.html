Fun-fact

I lived in this little town for a short time as a kid. In fact, this is where I started elementary school! Before that our family lived in Livermore, California. After joining the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints some good friends moved to Lava Hot Springs. Lava is another cute little Idaho town not far from Soda Springs. In the autumn, our family went out to visit them. Both of my parents had been born and raised in California and were so awed by the beautiful autumn colors (not exactly something you find in the "Golden State") that before the weekend was over, my mom found us a house and my dad secured a job. By Halloween we were packed up and made the move to Idaho.
